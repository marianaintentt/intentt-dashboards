Content Ideas
Based on what's working in travel rewards right now
IG + TT
"I redeemed X points for Y" — real redemption stories
Creator shows their actual redemption: business class seat, hotel night, or cash back — with the dollar value revealed at the end. Hook: "I paid $0 for this flight."
Why now: redemption content is outperforming educational content 3:1 on both platforms in the personal finance space.
TikTok
Side-by-side card comparison (scroll-stop format)
Two cards on screen, fast-cut comparison — earn rate, sign-up bonus, annual fee. No voiceover needed, just on-screen text. Works as a loop.
Why now: comparison content with no talking head is growing +40% on TikTok finance accounts Q2 2026.
Instagram
Carousel: "Best cards for [specific life event]"
Moving to a new city, getting married, first job, planning a baby. Hyper-specific angles with card recs per scenario. Very saveable = strong organic reach signal.
Why now: save rate on life-event carousels is 2–3x higher than generic "best cards" content. Saves predict subscriber growth.
IG + TT
"Points mistake I made" — anti-advice format
Creator admits a real mistake (wrong card for groceries, missed sign-up bonus, points expired). Builds trust fast. Comments will be extremely active — people share their own mistakes.
Why now: vulnerability/mistake content from finance creators is the highest comment-driver in the category right now.
TikTok
Reply to comments series — "you asked, here's the answer"
Pick the top question from comments each week, creator answers in a 30–45s video with the comment pinned on screen. Builds community, feeds the algorithm with replies.
Why now: TikTok is actively boosting reply-to-comment videos in the finance category as part of their creator retention push.
